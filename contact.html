Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping times?

We process orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the US. International shipping times vary by location.

Do you offer samples?

Yes! We offer sample sets of our fragrances. You can purchase a discovery set containing 5ml samples of our most popular scents.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Are your fragrances cruelty-free?

Yes, all our fragrances are cruelty-free and vegan. We're committed to ethical and sustainable practices in all aspects of our business.
