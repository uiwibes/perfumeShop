About Moonlight Serenade

Moonlight Serenade is a masterpiece of perfumery that captures the essence of a romantic evening under the stars. This enchanting fragrance is carefully crafted using the finest ingredients, blending traditional perfumery techniques with modern sophistication.

The journey begins with a bright burst of Italian bergamot and pink pepper, creating an intriguing opening that draws you in. The heart reveals a luxurious bouquet of night-blooming jasmine and vanilla orchid, complemented by the warmth of amber. As the fragrance develops, it settles into a rich base of sandalwood, musk, and a hint of Madagascar vanilla, creating a lasting impression that lingers on the skin.

The Experience

Each bottle of Moonlight Serenade is a work of art, housed in a hand-crafted crystal flacon that reflects the luminescent quality of moonlight. The fragrance evolves throughout the evening, revealing different facets of its complex composition as time passes, making it perfect for special occasions and memorable nights.
